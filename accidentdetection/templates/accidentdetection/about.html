About Accident Detection System

Accident Detection System is an application that uses computer vision and machine learning techniques to detect and classify road accidents in real time. The system can process live video feeds from cameras installed in different locations on the road network, and can detect various types of accidents, including collisions, rollovers, and other types of incidents.

The system uses deep learning algorithms to analyze video frames in real time, and can automatically generate alerts and notifications to emergency services, such as police, ambulance, and fire departments. The system can also provide real-time updates to traffic management centers and other stakeholders, helping them to quickly respond to accidents and minimize the impact on traffic flow and safety.
